## Telling the Time / Saat Söyleme

```
What time is it?
It is ...

7:00  → It is seven o'clock.
7:15  → It is quarter past seven.
7:30  → It is half past seven.
7:45  → It is quarter to eight.
```

## Simple Present Tense (Geniş Zaman)

Günlük rutinleri ve alışkanlıkları anlatmak için kullanılır.

| Subject | Verb | Example |
|---------|------|---------|
| I / You / We / They | V1 | I **get up** at 7. |
| He / She / It | V1 + s/es | She **gets up** at 7. |

### Example Paragraph:
> "I **wake up** at 7 o'clock every day. I **have breakfast** at 7:30. Then I **go to school**. I **have lunch** at 12:30. I **come home** at 3:00 and **do my homework**. I **go to bed** at 10:00."
